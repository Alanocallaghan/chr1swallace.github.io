Chris Wallace is a Wellcome Trust Senior Research Fellow, in the Department of Medicine and the MRC Biostatistics Unit at the University of Cambridge. These pages exist to store information about datasets and code used in papers from our group. The aim is to make this information accessible, which is not always possible in the published papers.



Our immune system protects us against infectious disease, but sometimes attacks our own cells, causing autoimmune disease. My group develop and apply statistical genomic methods to understand the genes, cells and pathways involved in the aetiology of autoimmune diseases such as type 1 diabetes and the relationships between different autoimmune diseases. Our goal is to further the understanding of these diseases in order to help prevent and treat them.
